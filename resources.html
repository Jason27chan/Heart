Resources

Effective Prevention Starts Here

Let's Talk

If you or someone you know is struggling with thoughts of suicide, reaching out is the first step.

Contact the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 1-800-273-8255.

Reach the Crisis Text Line by texting "START" to 741-741.

Text the Veterans Crisis Line by sending a message to 838255.

Life After Loss

You are not alone. Consider joining a support group to help you through this time of grief.

The Alliance of Hope and SOLOS (Survivors of Loved Ones to Suicide) host online support groups.

If you are interested in a local support group, the American Foundation for Suicide Prevention provides a service to search for a group near you. The AFSP also offers a Survivor Outreach Program.

Scheduling an appointment with a counselor or therapist can be a great help in improving mental wellness. You can find mental health providers in your area here.

Mental Health Care

Research has shown that a substantial portion of those who commit suicide suffer from mental illness. Fortunately, there are resources available for those living with mental illness.
